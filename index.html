Swami Pulp Packers - Excellence in Manufacturing

Located in the heart of Sangrur, Punjab, Swami Pulp Packers has been a pioneer in the packaging industry since 2003. We specialize in manufacturing high-quality, eco-friendly packaging solutions that cater to diverse industry needs.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with modern machinery and technology, enabling us to produce superior quality products efficiently. We take pride in our commitment to sustainability, using recyclable materials and environmentally responsible processes.

From apple trays to corrugated boards, egg trays to custom die-cut solutions, we offer a comprehensive range of packaging products. Our team of skilled professionals ensures that every product meets stringent quality standards.

At Swami Pulp Packers, we believe in building long-term relationships with our clients by providing reliable products, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service.
